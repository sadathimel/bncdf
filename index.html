Welcome to Bangladesh Non-Communicable Diseases Forum

Founded in 2009, Bangladesh Non-Communicable Diseases Forum has spread its wings, turning in the widening gyre of non-communicable diseases in Bangladesh with a vision to contribute to a community order where people live in an equitable and healthy environment with their maximum potential through prevention treatment, and control of NCDs. Being hooked up with more than 30 national organizations, BNCDF is on the move while acting jointly with INGOs, scientific and professional associations, academic and research institutions, private sectors, and dedicated individuals.

Urban Primary Health Care Services Delivery Project II

Since 1998, Bangladesh has been implementing Urban Primary Health Care Project. After three phases, the current project started in August 2019. The project aims to improve the health of urban residents, especially the poor, through enhancing Primary Health Care Services. At least 30% of project services are free of cost for the poor. The project is funded by GoB grant and ADB loan.
